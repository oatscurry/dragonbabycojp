What makes us "Dragonbaby"?

Dragonbaby believes that, when an artist creates, they cast into the deep river of imagination and pull out a living, breathing, original work.

It is the translator’s job to retrace the artist’s footsteps, find the same river of imagination, and pull out a work of equivalent energy and life.
